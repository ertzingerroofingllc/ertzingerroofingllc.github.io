About Us

A Full-Service Contracting Company

Ertzinger Roofing is a full-service contracting company that specializes in roofing, siding, gutters, and windows. We are passionate about all of the services we provide and are committed to giving our customers the best product. Unlike larger, corporate roofing companies in the area, we prioritize a customer-first approach. Rather than employing high-pressure sales tactics that can lead to customers overspending, we tailor our services to meet the individual needs of each client, whether for residential or commercial projects.

Helping Homeowners

In addition to our retail services, Ertzinger Roofing also specializes in helping homeowners and business owners with insurance claims related to storm damage. For those not looking to file an insurance claim and without the funds to pay out of pocket, but still want to get your project done, we also offer financing options to help get your project underway.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to providing the highest quality in all of our services. With our expertise and dedication to customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as the premier roofing company in Northeast Ohio.
